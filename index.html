About me

I finished my studies Building Engineering at Spain in 2012. I moved to France and did an internship of 6 months in a small studio renovating luxury villas in Cannes areas, mainly focused on interior design and the construction process. I moved to London 3 years ago and started working as an Architectural Technician/Assistant for 1 year directly for Landmark Lofts.

By mid-2015 I did a one-week intensive course on Revit and totally get hooked into it. To pursue my career goals I moved to work on a contract basis to have a flexible work schedule and be able to study at the same time I work. I've been since then working and undergoing an MSc in BIM Management which I expect to finish by November.

In case it is worth I am a certified Revit user and I am targetting to get the Professional Autodesk certification this year. I am also professionally experienced in AutoCAD and other CAD tools as detailed on my CV.

I am professionally fluent in Spanish, English and French.
